Association data

DMPred constructed the miRNA-disease bilayer network based on the known miRNA-disease associations. These associations were downloaded from the human miRNA disease database (HMDD, released in June 2014). After eliminating the redundant information, we get the valid association data.

Similarity data

The miRNA functional similarities calculated based on the associations of HMDD: miRNA similarity data.
The disease similarities calculated based on their directed acyclic graphs: disease similarity data .

Candidates data

The potential miRNA candidates associated with diseases, their ranking, and their association scores: candidates data.
