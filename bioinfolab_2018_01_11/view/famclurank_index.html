FamCluRank

A method for identifying disease-related microRNAs in the heterogeneous network with node attributes

Welcome to FamCluRank

Identification of disease related miRNAs (disease miRNAs) is an essential step towards discovering causal miRNAs and understanding disease pathogenesis. For a specific disease, FamCluRank can provide reliable candidate disease miRNAs which are worth further functional analysis in disease studies.
FamCluRank provides the candidate disease miRNAs for 329 diseases that include the well-characterized ones and the new ones. For a query disease d, FamCluRank lists its top 100 candidates, their ranks and association scores. The known d-related miRNAs are also demonstrated along with its candidates.
