DMPred — Disease-related MiRNAs Prediction

Identification of disease-related miRNAs (disease miRNAs) is critical for understanding disease etiology and pathogenesis.
First, DMPred can predict the disease miRNAs for more than 300 diseases and demonstrate them and their association scores.
Second, for a specific disease d, all the experimentally validated d-related miRNAs are also listed and they are labeled with 'known'.
Third, it provides the service of visualizing the associations between the multiple miRNAs and a specific disease with web Cytoscape.
