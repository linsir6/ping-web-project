Association data

FamCluRank constructed the miRNA-disease heterogeneous network with node attributes based on the known miRNA-disease associations. The associations were downloaded from the human miRNA disease database (HMDD, released in June 2014). After eliminating the redundant associations, we get the valid association data.

Similarity data

The miRNA functional similarities calculated based on the associations of HMDD: miRNA similarity data.
The disease similarities calculated based on their directed acyclic graphs: disease similarity data .

MiRNA families and clusters

The miRNAs and the miRNA families that they belong to: miRNA family data.
The miRNAs and the miRNA clusters that they belong to: miRNA cluster data.

Candidates data

The potential miRNA candidates associated with diseases, their ranking, and their association scores: candidates data.
